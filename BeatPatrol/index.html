How to play

Walk the beat. Get next to them.

Move anywhere on the street

Keys, click-to-walk, or touch-and-drag a joystick. Nothing can hurt you — there is nothing to dodge.

Stand next to an offender to catch them

No force, no contact — get adjacent and the case is made. The figure is a faceless silhouette with a case number.

BAIL GRANTED — most walk straight back

Off to the courthouse, then back down the street to the same shopfront, same case number. Catch them again.

CUSTODY — a few are held

That case is off your beat for the rest of the level. It never walks back. That’s the whole reward — and it’s the rare one.

Leave one loitering and the shop pays

Each shopfront goes open → reduced hours → shuttered on its own. The meter is all of them together.

The skill is who you go to next — not reflexes. Press F to head for the nearest offender.
